Lingua Sacra

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026
Zero Data Collection

Overview

Lingua Sacra is an educational app for learning Ecclesiastical Latin in the context of the Catholic tradition. Your privacy is important to us, and this app was designed from the ground up to respect it completely.

Data Collection

Lingua Sacra collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, the app does not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other information about you or your device.

Data Storage

All app data — including your lesson progress, quiz scores, vocabulary review schedule, and settings — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Activity

Lingua Sacra operates entirely offline. The app makes no network requests and does not communicate with any servers. No internet connection is required to use any feature of the app.

Third-Party Services

Lingua Sacra does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, social media, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Lingua Sacra is suitable for users of all ages. Because the app collects no data of any kind, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate any changes.
