Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this Classical or Ecclesiastical Latin?

Ecclesiastical (Church) Latin. This is the pronunciation used in the Vatican, in Catholic liturgy worldwide, and in sacred music. It differs from Classical Latin mainly in pronunciation — for example, “c” before “e” or “i” is pronounced “ch” (as in “charity”), not “k”.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, 100%. All lessons, flashcards, audio, prayers, and quizzes are included in the app. No internet connection is needed after you download it. Perfect for commutes, travel, or anywhere without reliable Wi-Fi.

Do I need any prior Latin knowledge?

Not at all. Lingua Sacra starts from absolute zero. If you do have some background, the built-in placement test lets you skip ahead to the right level so you’re not bored with basics you already know.

How is the pronunciation audio generated?

We use advanced AI text-to-speech specifically tuned for Ecclesiastical Latin pronunciation. Every word has been individually generated and reviewed to ensure it sounds authentic — the way you would hear it spoken in a Roman church.

How much does the app cost?

Lingua Sacra is a one-time purchase of $4.99 — no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads. You pay once and get the full app with all lessons, prayers, audio, and future updates. It was created as a labor of love to help Catholics engage with the Latin tradition of the Church.

What devices are supported?

Lingua Sacra is currently available on iPhone (iOS 15+). An Android version is coming soon. The app works on phones and tablets.

How does spaced repetition work?

Spaced repetition (SRS) is a proven learning technique. When you get a word right, the app waits longer before showing it again. When you struggle, it shows the word sooner. This optimizes your review time so you remember more with less effort.
